Calle Crisologo

Vigan City, Ilocos Sur

Calle Crisologo is a famous cobblestone street in Vigan City, Philippines, renowned for its remarkably well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture and ancestral houses.

#CalleCrisologo #ViganHeritage #IlocosSur #HistoricVigan #SpanishColonial

Bantay Bell Tower

Bantay, Ilocos Sur

This is an historic red-brick watchtower built in 1591, located on a hill near Vigan City. The tower's name (bantay means "to guard") comes from its original military use as a lookout against invaders. Today, it serves as the belfry for the adjacent St. Augustine Church and offers panoramic views.

#BantayBellTower #ViganView #ViganBelfry #TravelIlocos #HeritageSpot

Pinsal Falls

Pinsal, Ilocos Sur

This is the largest waterfall in the Ilocos Region, known for its 85-foot cascade and large, deep natural pools. It is also famous in local Ilocano mythology for having a pool that is said to be the footprint of the giant Angalo.
